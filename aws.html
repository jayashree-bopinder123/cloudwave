Amazon Web Services to Grow Business

Amazon is a pioneer in Cloud Computing and they keep rolling out newer version of their AWS service at regular intervals. AWS product categories range from pure IT resources and databases for storage, analysis, Artificial Intelligence, game development, and mobile services to IoT solutions. AWS as a whole belongs to a category called Infrastructure as a Service in Cloud Computing (IaaS).

At CloudWave, our AWS Certified Technicians work with you to design, migrate, secure, leverage, and optimize your AWS cloud so you can focus on growing your business while we take care of the infrastructural responsibilities. We will help you combine and match our Managed Cloud Services so you only pay for services just when you need them.

Why CloudWave for AWS?

CloudWave is an AWS consulting partner that enables you to improve your business processes through the intelligent use of cloud-based applications.

Our approach is all about building the best-in-class cloud solution for our customers. We take an impartial perspective to determine which cloud computing platform is optimal for building your solution.

AWS & CloudWave Benefits

Cost reduction and optimization

The need to reduce costs will likely impact your cloud migration strategy. We can periodically review your AWS configuration and look for ways to reduce costs and improve performance.

Migrations

Expertise

Finding and maintaining an internal experienced hand with AWS is a challenge. We have a team of AWS technical consultants, who can help you design and architect the AWS solution that best suit your business.

Security, & Compliance

Security and compliance are some of the main issues. We offer a solid combination of certified experts and management tools to protect your environment and the security and compliance of your data.

How We Work

Every project in the cloud is different. After evaluating your goals, we work with you to identify the cloud solution that best fits your business needs with a holistic approach that includes human resources, processes, and technology.
